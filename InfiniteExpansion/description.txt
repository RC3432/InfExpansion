Infinite Expansion Mod is a cheat tool mod used to alter massive amounts of terrain, delete, push, pull and freeze enemies,
and even create randomized blocks and items! The mod is small, but expect updates soon.. 

Items:

Deletion Zenith: Can instantly delete any NPC, projectile, item, and tile it touchs.
Heal Zenith: Instantly heals a player or NPC to maximum health.
Shrink and Expand Zenith: Reduces and Expands the size of projectiles, npcs, and items (When used).
Push and Pull Zenith: Drags and pushes away items, projectiles and npcs.
Create Zenith: Randomizes blocks, items, projectiles and npcs in a small area.
Tech Zenith: Nothing yet, expect more code soon :).
